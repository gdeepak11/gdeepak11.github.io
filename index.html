About Me

I am an Assistant Professor at IIIT Hyderabad since September 2019. Prior to this, I have been a Postdoctoral Researcher at University of Pennsylvania working on real-time systems and edge computing.

I obtained my PhD degree in School of Computing at National University of Singapore and was advised by leading researchers in real-time systems.

Prior to my PhD, I conducted research on Hardware-efficient reconfigurable architectures for image processing algorithms. I also worked as a Senior Design Engineer at Conexant Systems Inc., where I was involved in design and verification of ASIC and FPGA IP cores for optical communication protocols.

I obtained a Bachelor of Technology in Electronics and Communication from College of Engineering, Trivandrum, India.
